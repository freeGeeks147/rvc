Why Ahmedabad?

India's Sports Capital

Ahmedabad stands as a testament to India's sporting excellence and cultural diversity. With world-class infrastructure, passionate sports fans, and a rich heritage, we are ready to host the most memorable Commonwealth Games in history.

Our city combines ancient wisdom with modern innovation, creating the perfect backdrop for athletes from across the Commonwealth to compete, connect, and celebrate the spirit of sportsmanship.

From the iconic Sabarmati Riverfront to our state-of-the-art sports facilities, Ahmedabad offers an unparalleled experience that will leave lasting memories for athletes, officials, and spectators alike.
